ABOUT US

We aim to inspire everyone to experience the wonders of discoveries and ignite the passion of exploration of the world around us.

WHO WE ARE

We are a newly built community science museum who aim to ignite the passion of exploration specially for our intellectual and upcoming generation.

We have exciting exhibition which can be an inspiration for both adults and children. We are cooperating with nearby schools in the community, to push forward science as a means to create an arena for young people to develop their curious and intellectual mind.

We give back to the community with new discoveries to strengthen the culture of understanding and growth for our new generations.
